PostCard and Stamp

Creating a postcard and stamp featuring Highland Cows from Scotland is a delightful journey that combines creativity, local culture, and digital design. To craft a unique tourist postcard that captures the essence of the Scottish Highlands, I decided to draw inspiration from a specific town nestled amidst the stunning landscapes.My creative journey began with hand-drawn sketches. I wanted to capture the raw, rustic beauty of the Highland cattle and the serene backdrop of Braemar's natural splendor. The initial sketches allowed me to infuse personality into these shaggy, endearing creatures. Moving on to Adobe Illustrator, I explored various styles to bring my vision to life. The Highland Cows transitioned from hand-drawn sketches to digital illustrations, each stroke capturing the essence of their shaggy coats, majestic horns, and gentle eyes.
